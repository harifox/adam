We make solar easy.

We’ll take care of your entire solar project from start to finish.

Get a free solar consultation.

Every solar power project starts with a quick conversation to see if solar is right for you. We’ll discuss your energy use and take a look at your roof using satellite imaging.

We'll prepare a free customized quote if everything looks good. It includes all of your options and projects your energy savings for the next 20-30 years.

Sign your agreement.

Your personalized agreement secures your solar energy cost for the next 20 or 30 years, depending on your financing option. You’ll find it and the most important details of the agreement right on the front page.

There are no hidden fees, but you’ll find plenty of guarantees and warranties that protect you and your home.

Get a solar panel system design.

One of our site surveyors will visit within days of the solar consultation to take a closer look at your roof and take measurements.

Once we have the measurements, our expert engineers will design a solar power system based on your home’s dimensions and your energy needs.

Complete installation in 1 day.

Most solar panel system installations take only a single day. We’ll find a day that works best for you. We also take care of all of the permits and inspections. All you have to do is watch it all come together.

Turn on the power.

Here’s the best part. Once the utility company gives approval, you get to flip the switch to start generating your own clean, affordable energy.

Let’s put the plan into action.
